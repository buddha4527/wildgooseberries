Experience Tranquility in Thirunelly, Wayanad

Nestled amidst the lush greenery of Thirunelly, Wild Gooseberries Resort offers a serene escape in a beautifully restored traditional Kerala home. Just a short walk from the ancient Thirunelly Temple and the pristine Kalindi River, our resort provides a peaceful sanctuary for your mind and soul.

Enjoy **homely, authentic Kerala cuisine**, comfortable accommodations, and immerse yourself in nature at its very best. Our affordable and tranquil setting is perfect for families, couples, and anyone seeking a refreshing weekend getaway amidst Wayanad's captivating beauty.

Explore nearby trekking trails, unwind by the riverside, or simply relax in the serene ambiance of our garden. At Wild Gooseberries, every moment is an invitation to reconnect with yourself and nature.
