Wake up to birdsong, walk to the river, drink chai under mango trees, explore misty forest roads, and reconnect with a slower rhythm of life in Thirunelly, Wayanad.

Experience Wild Gooseberries

Nestled amidst the lush greenery of Thirunelly, Wild Gooseberries offers a serene escape in a beautifully restored traditional Kerala home and charming A-frame cottages.

Located close to Thirunelly Temple and the Kalindi River, our retreat is ideal for couples, families, backpackers, workations, and travelers seeking peace away from crowded tourism.

Enjoy authentic Kerala food, riverside walks, village experiences, and peaceful evenings surrounded by nature.
